We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets including Paytm, Google Pay, and PhonePe.

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for all plans. You can cancel anytime during the trial period without any charges.

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be prorated and reflected in your next billing cycle.

Yes! We provide comprehensive educational content and support for all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced traders.
