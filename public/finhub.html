Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our FinHub services and mentorship programs

What makes FinHub different from other trading education platforms?

FinHub is unique because we are SEBI Registered Research Analysts with proven track records. Our mentorship combines theoretical knowledge with practical application, real-time market analysis, and ongoing support. We focus on making you independent rather than dependent on our recommendations.

How long does it take to become an independent trader?

The timeline varies based on your dedication and prior knowledge. Most students start seeing results within 3-6 months of consistent practice. Our structured approach ensures you build a strong foundation before moving to advanced strategies.

What support do I get after completing the mentorship?

You get lifetime access to our community, weekly market updates, quarterly portfolio reviews, and ongoing mentorship support. We believe in building long-term relationships with our students.

Are the portfolio recommendations suitable for beginners?

Yes, our portfolios are designed with different risk profiles in mind. We offer conservative, balanced, and aggressive options. Each portfolio comes with detailed explanations and risk assessments to help you make informed decisions.

How do I access the swing trade ideas and updates?

You get access through our secure portal, WhatsApp updates, email notifications, and text messages. We also provide weekly trade review summaries and missed-trade re-entry alerts to maximize your opportunities.
